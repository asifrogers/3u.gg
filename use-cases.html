Use Cases


Short URLs prove convenient in various digital communication and marketing contexts. Here are some common use cases for URL shortening:

YouTube Channel Links

You might want to shorten your YouTube links to save space in emails or social media posts. Long and cumbersome YouTube links are hard to remember and usually take up more space on web pages, emails, and social media communication.

Maps

Shortened URLs to maps make it easier to share with others. Long URLs are often hard to memorize and may not fit well in images due to their length. A short URL like 3u.gg/loc fits anywhere and is easy to remember, even from a flyer or brochure.

Webpages

Long URLs for websites can be cumbersome to read and type. Short URLs simplify the process, making them easier to handle and share. They are more user-friendly and fit better in text fields or on printed materials, enhancing usability.

Restaurant Locations

Short URLs provide a more efficient way to share information about restaurant locations and websites. Simplifying the link structure allows customers easy access to menus, special offers, or reservation details.

Calendly

Calendly is a popular scheduling tool for businesses. It helps schedule meetings. However, it's even more important how the link looks when you share it through email. Use 3u.gg to easily cloak Calendly links.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn profile URLs are often lengthy and tedious to include in resumes or share with employers. Shortening your LinkedIn URL creates a cleaner, more professional appearance in job applications.
