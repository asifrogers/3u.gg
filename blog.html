The trouble stems from the fact that any “Call-to-Action” links or URLs on spreadable media are non-clickable and there is no way to implement detailed tracking, no way to ascertain which campaign resulted in the end-user reaching its target. This is where 3u.gg comes in.

Campaign and Behavior Tracking Separation

3u.gg helps in separating behavior tracking from campaign tracking by embedding all campaign tracking parameters into a short-URL and booking the values of those parameters initially when the short-URL is generated.

This way it enables an advertiser to continue getting useful results from its campaign tracking even if behavior tracking parameters are blocked.
