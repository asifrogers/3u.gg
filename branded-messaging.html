What are Mnemonic URLs?

Safety cautious customers prefer to type the links instead of clicking on them. Are your shortened URLs optimized for typing? Are the URLs Mnemonic?

Mnemonic URLs are short URLs optimized for typing by hand. In short they are:
Case Insensitive

URLs work regardless of capitalization; /Mfa, /MFA, and /mfa are treated the same.

Mistake Tolerant

Handles similar-looking characters seamlessly, such as (/T0T is same as TOT, and BIT same as BlT).

Intentionally Short

URLs are concise, with only a few characters, unless deliberately expanded using dictionary words.
